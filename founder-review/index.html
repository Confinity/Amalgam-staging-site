How it runs

How the engagement is structured

The structure stays disciplined so the work keeps moving toward one useful outcome instead of spreading into a broad, vague engagement.

01

Plan the review

We align on which systems, teams, and questions are in scope for the time we have.

02

Gather the facts

Interviews, document and code review, and a pass across delivery and architecture where relevant.

03

Synthesize and rank options

04

Hand off the brief

You get a short written read and, if useful, a follow-up call to walk leadership through the findings.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Short answers to the questions teams usually ask while deciding whether this is the right level of support.

When should we start with Founder Review instead of Project Execution?+

Start with Founder Review when the work product you need is an assessment: agreed facts, options, and a recommendation. Start with Project Execution when the scope of work is already clear enough to staff and run against milestones.

What is in the deliverable?+

A written read of the situation, a short list of options, and what we would do next in your shoes. The format is tuned to how your team makes decisions today.

What do you need from us to begin?+

A point of contact, access to the right people for interviews, and read access to the systems and docs we agree are in scope.

Need an assessment before the next commit?

We will confirm whether a Founder Review is the right format and what to include in scope.
