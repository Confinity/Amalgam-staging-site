Overview

AI Readiness

Use this when you are trying AI but not seeing meaningful change yet.

Most efforts stay at the demo stage and do not change how work gets done.

This helps you focus on the few use cases that change how work gets done.

When to use

  • AI efforts feel scattered
  • Experiments are not leading to results
  • There's pressure to "do something with AI"
  • You want impact, not noise

What's inside

  • how to assess if your team is ready for AI
  • how to pick use cases that matter
  • how to test value before scaling
  • how to set guardrails early
  • how to roll out without disruption
