How you can engage with us

Recommended first step

01—Founder Review

For solo founders and early teams

Get a clear picture of what's working, what's not, and what to do next. We review your product, strategy, and systems, then give you a simple, prioritized plan.

  • Clear breakdown of what's blocking progress
  • A focused plan you can actually execute
  • Direction you can use for hires, budget, or next steps

Best when · You're making an important decision but don't fully trust the direction yet

Typical scope · 1–2 weeks

02—Project Execution

For teams with a defined outcome

You know what needs to get built—we help you ship it. We scope the work, execute against milestones, and deliver something real your team can use.

  • Clear plan with milestones and owners
  • Working product, features, or systems delivered
  • Smooth handoff to your internal team

Best when · You have a clear goal and need it built fast and correctly

Typical scope · 4–10 weeks

03—Growth Partnership

For teams building continuously

A dedicated team that stays with you as you grow. Instead of restarting every project, we stay embedded and move across your roadmap with you.

  • Ongoing support across multiple projects
  • Faster execution since context is never lost
  • One consistent team, not new vendors every time

Best when · You have ongoing work and need consistency + speed

Typical scope · 3+ months
